Getting started

Before beginning the tuning process for your Xiaomi 4 Pro 2nd Generation, 5, 5 Pro, or 5 Max, please ensure that the following requirements are met.
A Laptop/PC running Windows
A compatible adapter - Click here

Links

Please ensure that you open the following links before continuing with this documentation.
  • Website 1. Scooter Firmware: https://mi-fw-info.streamlit.app
  • Website 2. Brightway Patcher: https://bw-patcher.streamlit.app
  • Website 3. Brightway Flasher: https://github.com/scooterteam/bw-flasher/releases/download/v0.5.1/bwflasher-v0.5.1-windows-x64.exe
Connect the Scooter with the your Laptop / Pc

Step 1: Open the handlebar assembly of the scooter.
Carefully detach the handlebar from the main body to access the internal connection.

Step 2: Disconnect the handlebar cable.
Locate the cable connecting the handlebar to the scooter’s main unit and gently unplug it.

Step 3: Connect the adapter.
Attach your adapter to the designated port on the bottom side of the scooter’s main body (do not connect it to the handlebar).

Step 4: Establish USB connection.
Plug the other end of the adapter into your computer using a USB-C port.

First step - Website 1

1.

Pick your Scooters firmware

After identifying your scooter model, download the appropriate firmware. For example, if you own a Xiaomi 5 Pro scooter, search for:
“Xiaomi Electric Scooter 5 Pro”.

2.

Download the Firmware

Locate and download the appropriate MCU firmware specific to your scooter model. Ensure that the firmware version matches your scooter to avoid compatibility issues.

Note: Using incorrect firmware may cause malfunctions or permanently damage the device. Always verify the model and version before proceeding.

The second step - Website 2

1.

Upload the Firmware

1. Upload the firmware file you previously downloaded to the Brightway Patcher website.
2. Once the upload is complete, select your scooter model from the list.

Example: If you are using the Xiaomi 5 Pro, choose mi5pro from the available options.

2.

Patching the Firmware 

You can now choose from the following available modifications:
w
SLS – Sets the maximum speed in Sport Mode.


SLD – Sets the maximum speed in Default Mode.


RFM – Enable this option if it is available.


FDV – This option is not required and can be left unchanged.


After adjusting the desired settings, click the Download button at the bottom of the page to save your modified firmware.

Tip: If you are unsure which values to choose, refer to the example image provided and copy the recommended settings on the Image.

The last step - Website 3

Now you have to Download Brightway flasher from Github, after downloading it open the file

1.

Run Brightway Flasher

1. Locate the firmware file you downloaded and double-click to open it.

2. Wait for the confirmation window to appear.

3. Once the window is displayed, click OK to proceed.

2.

Flash the Scooter

1. Click the Browse button and select the firmware file you patched in the previous step.

2. Enable Debug Mode by checking the corresponding box.

3. Click Test Connection to verify that your device is properly connected.

4. If the connection test is successful, click Start Update to begin the firmware update process.
